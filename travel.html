Words on Wander: 2022 - 2023

Seattle

Witnessed one of the world's best literary scene at the AWP conference from keynote speakers to inspiring panel discussions, readings, and shared passions.

Sleeping Bear Dunes, Michigan

This enchanting landscape of towering sand dunes, pristine beaches, and crystal-clear lakes is a tranquil escape into the heart of the Great Lakes region.

Cape Cod

Lost myself in the idyllic beauty of Cape Cod, Massachusetts with its picturesque lighthouses, serene beaches, and charming seaside villages. Truly a coastal gem.

New York

"New York is not a city, it's a world." - Iman. My smartwatch told me that I'd walked over a 40,000 steps in two days. Lived the scenes from Kal ho Na Ho â€“ around Dumbo & Brooklyn Bridge.


Gatlinburg

I roamed the charming streets of Gatlinburg, Tennessee, went apple cider tasting, watched the rolling blue peaks of the Blue Ridge Mountains and came back with a bucketload of adventure!

Boston

Went ice skating for the first time, explored new brewries, gallivanted along the seaport and celebrated Christmas with family. Couldn't have asked for a better start to the new year!

Niagra Falls

I heard the waters roar! From the Cave of the Winds to the Maid of the Mist, the majesty and wonder od Niagra Falls is a testament to the power of nature.

Chicago

Exploring the Windy City's streets and architecture, Chicago stole a pice of my heart! From the iconic Bean in Millenium Park, the Navy Pier to the magnificent skyline along Lake Michigan, I loved it!
