About me

I am a passionate content creator and editor driven by a deep love for words and storytelling.

Currently, I study Creative Writing (MFA) at Miami University.

My journey began with a Bachelor's degree in English Literature, where I delved into literary worlds and honed my writing skills. Eager to explore the intersection of creativity and business, I pursued a Master's in Business Administration, adding a strategic layer to my creative foundation.

Beyond the world of words, I am an avid traveler, finding inspiration in the diverse cultures and landscapes I encounter. Whether I'm exploring bustling city streets or serene natural wonders, my travels infuse my writing with a unique worldly perspective.

In addition to my creative pursuits, I am a practitioner of spiritualism, finding profound meaning in the connection between the self and the universe.

To learn more about my academic and professional background, please feel free to download my resume. I look forward to sharing my expertise and creativity with you.
